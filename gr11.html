Eleventh Grade

Title: "Bouquet of Joy"

Focus: The careful details found in flowers

Medium: Water colour

Notes from the artist: One of my favourite things to draw are flowers because every fold creates so many dynamic shades of red in a rose.

Title: "Pinball Party"

Medium: clay, pencil crayon, construction paper, wood, paint

Inspiration: Charles Bell - an incredible still life painter with extreme care for details and bright colours.

Notes from the Artist: The background of the board contains facts about Charles Bell. I always tried to add a little more in my art pieces in school. The addition of wood was unique amongst my classmates; they stuck to using only the clay with which we were assigned to create something. Believe me when I say I was extremely excited to see this design come to life!
