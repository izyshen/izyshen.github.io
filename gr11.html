Title: Bouquet of Joy

Medium: Watercolour

Inspiration: I wanted to immortalize the beauty of flowers. Their subtle scents, the vibrant colours and the assiciated feelings of love and gratitude induce smiles on the faces of many.

Title: A Pinball Tea Party

Medium: Clay

Inspiration: A reinterpretation of artwork by Charles Bell but in the form of objects for a tea party. Created as a class project.

Title: There Exists an End to Childhood

Medium: Oil pastel, chalk pastel

Inspiration: As I was approaching university, everyone wanted to know what I wanted to be. Like most of my friends, I didn't know. This piece was created to acknowledge and pay tribute to those "ending" moments because in a general aspect, we all face many of those. What's important is how we handle our next steps.
